What can you do in Foodie?

Search over 1,000,000 recipes

Bookmark them, add recipes to your weekly meal planner and select what ingredients you need to buy. They will be waiting for you in your shopping list!

Add your favorite recipes

Give us an URL of your favorite recipe on the Internet, an URL of its image and some more details. We will keep your dearest recipes safe and handy!

Personalise your shopping list

Personal or recipe-selected ingredients can be gathered in your easy-editable shopping list. You choose its title, the items and their order. Download it or check the ingredients in the app.

Find all your future meals in one planner

Take time every monday to think about your nutritional goals for the next week. Plan you meals carefully and never ask yourself again "What should I eat today?"
